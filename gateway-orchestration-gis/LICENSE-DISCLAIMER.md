# Legal Disclaimer — Woodfine Location Intelligence

The Woodfine Location Intelligence platform (gis.woodfinegroup.com) provides synthesized location intelligence metrics on an "as is" basis.

## 1. Metric Synthesis

Scores, rankings, and spend estimates are computed from statistical models applied to open-data inputs. These metrics are designed for regional market analysis and trend identification, not for high-precision measurement.

## 2. No Guarantee

Scores are derived from multiple authoritative sources; Woodfine Group does not guarantee the accuracy of specific retailer coordinates or synthesized economic metrics. Data on this platform is for research and market analysis only.

## 3. Privacy & Ethics

No individual-level tracking or personally identifiable information (PII) is used in the generation of mobility or demographic layers. All mobility data is derived from synthetic trajectories (WorldMove) or anonymized, aggregated visitor patterns.

## 4. Usage Restrictions

This platform is not intended for:

- Real-time navigation or logistics routing.
- Critical infrastructure planning or emergency response.
- High-stakes site selection without independent field verification.
